Making Brownies

This recipe explains how to make brownies.

Prerequisites

  • Chocolate Chips

  • Cocoa Mixture

  • Eggs

  • Milk

  • Flour

  • Sugar

  • Vanilla Extract

  • Mixing Bowl

  • Spatula

  • Whisk

Procedure

  1. Take out all the ingredients for making brownies.

  2. Preheat oven to 350F (175C).

  3. Mix the butter, sugar and vanilla together.

  4. Add the eggs, one at a time. Pour in milk slowly.

  5. Add the flour, chocolate chips and cocoa mixture.

  6. Whisk the mixture well.

  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes.
